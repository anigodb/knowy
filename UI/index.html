Remote Work Policy

Canonical answer

Full content

Effective: June 2026

This policy outlines the remote work guidelines for all employees. Remote work is defined as any work performed outside of a company office location.

Standard Arrangement: Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remaining days should be spent in the office unless otherwise arranged with your manager.

Extended Remote: Requests for more than 3 days per week require manager approval and must be submitted through the HR portal.
